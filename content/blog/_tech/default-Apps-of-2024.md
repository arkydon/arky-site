## Default apps of 2024

- Browser: Moved from **Kiwi** to **Microsoft Edge** because Kiwi is no longer updated. 

- Notes: Switched from **Notion** to **Upnote** until I find a bteer notes app because it was becoming too laggy

- Weather: Switched from **Weather Timeline** to **Today Weather** which is almost as good as Weather Timeline. Got a premium code for free from the devs.

- Gallery: I'm using two Gallery Apps - **Fossify** and **FStop**. 

- **Youtube Music** for Music streaming because it comes free with Youtube Premium . I like it but I would pick **Spotify** if I had to choose between these two.

- **Honista IG Mod**: Switched to this Instagram mod because the previous. **MyInsta** mod hasn't been updated in a long time but I need to find a better alternative because the Honista dev added his own ads to the app which is a huge red flag.
